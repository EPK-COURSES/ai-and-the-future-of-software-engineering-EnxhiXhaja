- Topic 8: Real Industry Case Studies (Google)

After researching how Google uses AI-assisted coding tools, I learned that AI is already an important part of software development in real companies. It is not something from the future but something that engineers already use in their daily work.

What surprised me most is that Google has its own internal AI tools like Codey and other systems based on Gemini. I thought AI tools were only public tools like Copilot but big companies also build their own systems to help their developers.

One thing I am a bit concerned about is that developers may depend too much on AI. Even if AI is helpful engineers still need to understand the code and check if it is correct especially in important systems.
At the same time, I see many good sides. AI can help reduce simple and repetitive tasks, help new developers understand code faster and improve productivity in large projects.

I think the most important skills in the future will still be problem solving, understanding systems and being able to think critically. These skills cannot be replaced easily by AI.

In my opinion AI will not replace software engineers. Instead, it will work as a tool that helps them do their job better and faster.

This research helped me understand that software engineering is changing and developers need to adapt and learn how to work with AI tools .
